Why not WordPress?

or any other regular "free" blogging website?

This blog was my very first project since I was discharged from the military. I wanted to start fresh, and decided that I would need a place where I could document my progress as a programmer and also some other plans I had in my mind. And I didn't want to just "drag and drop" to create my site. I wanted something of my own. Using basic html,css and also both javascript and jquery, I was able to craft the front-end part of the blog, and used Google Firebase as my back-end part (I believe Firebase was the best option for me to learn about back-end as a beginner). I admit that this blog may not be the best looking nor the most mobile friendly site, but I am still learning and this too will just be part of my progress. All the deatils regarding my projects will be posted in my blog.
